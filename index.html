Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2025

Information We Collect

  • Email: Required for registration and access to advanced features.
  • Usage data: Tools used and frequency of use.
  • Images and messages: Temporarily stored for analysis and personalized response generation.
  • Device data: Device model, operating system, and IP address.

Camera and Image Usage

VibeAI requires access to the camera to analyze images and messages to generate personalized responses. Images and messages are processed within the app and are not permanently stored on our servers.

Facial Analysis

If you choose to use the facial analysis feature, VibeAI may temporarily process images that contain a face. These images are used solely to generate contextual responses and suggestions (such as personal care or makeup tips). We do not collect or create biometric identifiers, faceprints, or facial recognition templates. Processing occurs securely and only in temporary memory for the duration of the analysis. The images are not permanently stored on our servers or on the device once the analysis is completed, and they are not shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate natural and contextual responses based on the provided images and messages.
  • Offer suggestions to improve appearance through facial analysis.
  • Personalize recommendations and tools for a better user experience.
  • Develop new features and improve the accuracy of AI-generated responses.
  • Send updates and relevant notifications.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties, and we never share face data. Face data is processed only within the app and deleted immediately after use. Other personal information (such as email or usage data) may be shared with service providers strictly as needed to operate the app (for example, subscription management or secure cloud storage).

  • Service providers: We may use external services for secure cloud storage of non-facial data (e.g., email, account data).
  • Legal compliance: If required by law or a court order.

Where We Store Your Information

Your personal information (such as email and account data) is securely stored on protected servers using encryption and other security measures to prevent unauthorized access. Face data is never stored and exists only temporarily in memory during analysis, after which it is permanently deleted.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your account and personal data by emailing contact@vibeai.com.
  • Withdraw consent for camera usage or facial analysis at any time from the app settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: contact@vibeai.com
